üí° Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • What is COPD? Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) is a group of lung diseases that block airflow and make it difficult to breathe.
  • What are the symptoms of COPD? Symptoms include chronic cough, shortness of breath, wheezing, and mucus production.
  • How can I manage COPD? COPD can be managed through medication, lifestyle changes, and pulmonary rehabilitation. Early diagnosis is key to better management.
  • Can COPD be cured? COPD is not curable, but it can be managed effectively with proper treatment and lifestyle changes.
